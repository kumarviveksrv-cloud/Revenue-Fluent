Before you upload

You are about to upload employee data. Please read this first.

Revenue Fluent processes your data entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. However, the data you upload likely contains sensitive personal information about real people. You must confirm you have the right to use it here.
⚠ Anonymise your data before uploading
Replace employee names with anonymous IDs (e.g. EMP001, EMP002) in the Employee ID column before uploading. Do not include full names, national ID numbers, contact details, or any data that would directly identify an individual. This protects your employees and reduces your compliance risk significantly.
🔒 How your data is handled
Your data never leaves your browser. It is stored in sessionStorage, a temporary browser memory that is permanently and automatically deleted the moment you close this tab. It is not cached, not saved to disk, and not transmitted anywhere. Revenue Fluent has no server, no database, and no access to anything you upload.
