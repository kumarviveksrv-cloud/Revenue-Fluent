Organizational Enablement Index
ViRorah Metric #9 · Compensation Lens
OEI
The structural revenue metric. OEI measures whether your org's layer structure enables or throttles revenue generation. Too top-heavy = execution slows = revenue drops. This is the single most powerful indicator of Configuration C drift.
Formula
OEI = (Revenue-Generating Roles ÷ Total Headcount) × 100

Revenue-generating = roles with direct output accountability
Non-revenue = management, coordination, support layers
65%+
Healthy (lean)
50-65%
Watch zone
Below 50%
Top-heavy critical

Organizational Stability Index
ViRorah Metric #13 · Resilience Lens
OSI
Team stability = revenue predictability. OSI measures how stable your team configurations are over time. Two restructures in 6 months is an OSI nightmare – and a revenue predictability disaster.
Formula
OSI = 100 - (Number of Structural Changes × 10)

Structural changes: restructures, reorgs, role redesigns,
reporting line changes, team splits or merges
80+
Stable
60-80
Some disruption
Below 60
Unstable – revenue at risk

Workforce Gap Index
ViRorah Metric #17 · Capacity Lens
WGI
The gap between your planned team structure and your actual team structure. WGI reveals where your Revenue DNA is being starved – where the right roles are missing, unfilled, or filled with the wrong archetype.
Formula
WGI = ((Planned Headcount - Actual Effective Headcount) ÷ Planned) × 100

Effective headcount = roles filled at full productive capacity
(Not just filled – productive capacity weighted)
Below 10%
Healthy gap
10-20%
Meaningful gap
Above 20%
Revenue capacity hole
