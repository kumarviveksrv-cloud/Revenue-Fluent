ViRorah Metric #14 · Resilience Lens
IRD
How deeply is critical knowledge embedded across your org? IRD measures whether your company's core capabilities live in a few people's heads or are genuinely distributed. Low IRD = one exit destroys institutional capability.
IRD = (Documented processes / Total critical processes) × (Cross-trained people / Total role-critical people) × 100
