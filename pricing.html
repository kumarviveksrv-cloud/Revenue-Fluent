Frequently asked
Is this a course or a tool?▼
It's both — deliberately. The tools are live calculators you use on your real org data. The learning is deep — each pillar has its intellectual case, real stories from India and global markets, worked examples, and a practitioner's playbook. The Simulation and Case Studies make you practice decisions under pressure. Nothing about this platform is passive.
I'm not a data person. Can I use this?▼
Yes. Revenue Fluent was built for HR practitioners, not data scientists. Every tool has a plain-English narrative that explains what the numbers mean. The Learning Hub doesn't assume any prior analytics knowledge. If you can read a P&L, you can use this.
What does "one-time" actually mean?▼
Pay once, access forever. No subscription, no renewal, no surprise charges. The CRFP Individual plan is a single payment that gives you lifetime access to everything — including new scenarios when they're added sporadically. If this platform ever shuts down (it won't, but if it did), you'd have had full access for as long as it existed.
What's the difference between CRFP Individual and PRO Monthly?▼
The content is identical. The difference is payment model. CRFP Individual is ₹3,999 once — done, access forever. PRO Monthly is ₹499/month — useful if you prefer spreading the cost, or if you want to cancel after a month. If you're planning to actually use this, CRFP Individual is better value within 8 months.
Is CRFP a recognised certification?▼
CRFP (Certified Revenue Fluent Practitioner) is a Revenue Fluent certification — not a university or professional body credential. What it signals is specific: you can build a People P&L, you understand 18 revenue metrics, and you can present HR's contribution in the language of the CFO. In a board meeting or job interview, that capability speaks for itself — regardless of who issued the certificate.
Can I use this for my team or org?▼
Individual licences are for individual use. If you want to put your whole HR team through Revenue Fluent, reach out directly — team pricing can be arranged. The tools are designed to work on real org data, so a team going through this together with your actual numbers is genuinely powerful.
