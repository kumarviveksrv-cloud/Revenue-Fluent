Frequently Asked Questions
Is the Free tier actually free — or is it a trial?
Actually free. No trial period, no card required, no expiry. All five tools, all 15 scenarios, Show Workings, HCI — permanently free. The paid tiers add the institutional toolkit: Excel models you take back to your org, PDF reports you bring to the boardroom, your own data, and the CRFP credential.
What is the CRFP certification?
The Certified Revenue Fluent Practitioner (CRFP) is the professional credential for HR practitioners who can speak the language of Revenue. It demonstrates that you understand the Humacity philosophy, can translate people decisions into financial language, and can defend those numbers in a boardroom setting. It is built on the same intellectual architecture as Revenue Fluent itself.
Why Gumroad? Why not a card on the platform?
Revenue Fluent is built by a solo practitioner for HR practitioners. Gumroad handles payments, receipts, and delivery securely without requiring server infrastructure. It also means your payment data never touches this platform — it stays with Gumroad's PCI-compliant payment system.
Can I use Revenue Fluent for client work?
Yes. The CRFP Individual tier is designed for consultants and advisors using Revenue Fluent with clients. The Excel models and PDF reports are yours to use in client engagements. The PRO Monthly tier adds multi-org data upload for practitioners running multiple engagements simultaneously.
Is my data private?
Your data never leaves your browser. The CSV upload feature processes your employee data entirely client-side — nothing is sent to any server. Revenue Fluent has no backend and no database. The only data stored is your practitioner profile preferences in your browser's localStorage.
