Frequently Asked Questions
Is the Free tier actually free, or is it a trial?
Actually free. No trial period, no card required, no expiry. All five tools, all 15 scenarios, Show Workings, HCI permanently free. PRO adds the institutional toolkit: Excel models, PDF reports, and your own data.
Why are there two PRO prices?
Purchasing power parity. Rs 999 and $12 are roughly equivalent in value for their respective markets. The features are identical. Choose based on your billing location.
Why Gumroad?
Revenue Fluent is built by a solo practitioner for HR practitioners. Gumroad handles payments, receipts, and delivery securely. Your payment data never touches this platform.
Is my data private?
Your data never leaves your browser. The CSV upload feature processes everything client-side. Nothing is sent to any server. Revenue Fluent has no backend and no database.
Can I use Revenue Fluent for client work?
Yes. PRO is designed for consultants and advisors using Revenue Fluent with clients. The Excel models and PDF reports are yours to use in client engagements.
