Total Compensation Growth Delta
ViRorah Metric #8 · Compensation Lens
TCGD
Is your compensation growing faster or slower than your revenue? TCGD reveals whether people cost is compounding as a liability or being managed as a strategic lever.
TCGD = ((This year total comp - Last year total comp) / Last year total comp) - ((This year revenue - Last year revenue) / Last year revenue)
Positive TCGD = comp growing faster than revenue (danger)
Negative TCGD = revenue growing faster than comp (healthy leverage)
