Scaling Pace Efficiency Index
ViRorah Metric #7 · Compensation Lens
SPEI
As your org grows, does revenue per person increase or decrease? SPEI measures whether you're gaining leverage as you scale — or bleeding efficiency through headcount bloat.
Formula
SPEI = (Revenue Growth % ÷ Headcount Growth %) × 100

SPEI > 100 → Revenue growing faster than headcount (gaining leverage)
SPEI = 100 → Revenue and headcount growing at same rate (neutral)
SPEI < 100 → Headcount growing faster than revenue (losing leverage)

ViRorah Metric #12 · Volatility Lens

How fast are you making hiring decisions vs. the market? Faster HCM = faster revenue activation. Slow HCM means losing top candidates to competitors and extending revenue capacity gaps.
Formula
HCM = Benchmark Days-to-Hire ÷ Your Avg Days-to-Hire × 100

HCM < 100 → Slower than market (disadvantage)
