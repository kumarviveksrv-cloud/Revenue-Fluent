Attrition Cost %
ViRorah Metric #5 · Leakage Lens
ATC%
Every exit costs far more than the salary. Recruitment, onboarding, lost productivity, knowledge drain, team morale impact – most HR pros only count the recruitment fee. This metric counts everything.
Formula
ATC% = (Total Exit Cost ÷ Annual Salary) × 100

Total Exit Cost = Recruitment Cost + Onboarding Cost
                    + Lost Productivity Cost + Pipeline/Knowledge Loss
Recruitment Cost
Agency fees + job board + interview time. Typically 15-20% of salary.
Onboarding Cost
Training investment + manager time + buddy allocation. 8-12% of salary.
Lost Productivity
Ramp period at 50% capacity × monthly salary × ramp months. Largest component.
Pipeline / Knowledge Loss
Revenue-generating roles: deals in flight, client relationships, institutional knowledge.

Talent Disruption Frequency
ViRorah Metric #6 · Leakage Lens
TDF
A team can look "fully staffed" on paper while being in complete revenue-destroying turmoil underneath. TDF reveals the chaos that headcount reports never show.
Formula
TDF = Total Significant People Events ÷ (Team Size × Quarters Measured)

Significant events: Exits · Unplanned hires · Promotions out of role
                      · Restructures · Role changes affecting output
Healthy TDF
0.1 - 0.3 per team per quarter. Stable team, predictable output.
Warning TDF
0.3 - 0.6. Some disruption. Revenue consistency at risk.
Critical TDF
0.6+. High chaos. Revenue predictability severely compromised.
Why it matters
Every 0.1 increase in TDF correlates with ~8% reduction in team revenue output.

Execution Stability Ratio
ViRorah Metric #11 · Volatility Lens
ESR
Of your total team, how many people are actually at full productive capacity RIGHT NOW? A team of 10 with 3 ramping, 1 exiting, and 1 newly promoted is running at roughly 60% capacity – even though headcount shows 10.
Formula
ESR = (Effective Productive Capacity ÷ Total Potential Capacity) × 100

Capacity weights by status:
 Fully ramped & stable → 100%
 Ramping (0-3 months) → 25%
 Ramping (3-6 months) → 50%
 Exit notice period → 20%
 Recently promoted (0-3 months) → 40%
