The complete reversal.

Every other function presents a P&L to justify its budget.
Marketing: "We spent ₹2Cr. We generated ₹18Cr. ROI: 9x."
Sales: "We cost ₹4Cr. We closed ₹42Cr. ROI: 10.5x."
HR presents headcount and engagement scores.
The board tolerates it. Nobody challenges it. But nobody takes it seriously either.
The People P&L ends this completely.
HR: "We generated ₹21Cr in People Revenue. We cost ₹28.6Cr. The gap is the succession decision — here's the fix."
That's not an HR presentation. That's a CFO presentation.
