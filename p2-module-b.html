How to Calculate Each Alpha

Each of the 5 Alpha dimensions has a specific calculation methodology – a formula, a set of inputs, and a benchmark range. These are the methodologies that make People Alpha measurable, not just conceptual.

Hiring Alpha
Uses: BEE (#2) + HCR (#18)
Hiring Alpha = ((Actual Revenue per New Hire Y1 - Benchmark Revenue per New Hire Y1) ÷ Benchmark) × 100

Where: Benchmark = industry median Year 1 revenue per equivalent role
+10%+
Strong
-5% to +10%
Average
Below -5%
Weak
Sterling Q1: 4 new hires (promoted consultants) → Year 1 revenue/hire: £54,000 vs benchmark £142,000 → Hiring Alpha: -62% 🔴
Promotion Alpha
Uses: SPEI (#7) + ESR (#11)
Promotion Alpha = ((Post-Promo Team Revenue Delta - Benchmark Delta) ÷ Benchmark Delta) × 100

Where: Benchmark Delta = industry average team revenue change in 6 months post-promotion
+8%+
Strong
0% to +8%
Average
Below 0%
Destroying value
Sterling: Team revenue 6 months post-promotion: -18% vs benchmark +8% → Promotion Alpha: -31% 🔴
Retention Alpha
Uses: Attrition Rate (#4) + BEE (#2)
Retention Alpha = ((Avg Revenue/Person at Tenure T - Benchmark Revenue/Person at T) ÷ Benchmark) × 100

Where: T = tenure cohort (Year 2, Year 3, Year 4+)
+15%+
Strong
0% to +15%
Average
Below 0%
Culture not compounding
Sterling: Senior consultants (3yr+ tenure) bill 34% more than Year 1 → vs benchmark 26% → Retention Alpha: +8% 🟢
Team Design Alpha
Uses: OEI (#9) + PCoB (#1)
Team Design Alpha = ((Actual Revenue/FTE - Benchmark Revenue/FTE) ÷ Benchmark) × 100

Where: Benchmark = industry median revenue per FTE for comparable team structures
+10%+
Strong
-5% to +10%
Average
Below -5%
Org design leaking value
Sterling: Revenue/FTE = £148,000 vs benchmark £175,000 → Team Design Alpha: -15% (too partner-heavy) 🔴
L&D Alpha
Uses: SPEI (#7) + HCM (#12)
L&D Alpha = ((Attributable Revenue from L&D / L&D Spend) - Benchmark ROI) ÷ Benchmark ROI × 100

Where: Benchmark ROI = industry median revenue generated per £1 of L&D investment
